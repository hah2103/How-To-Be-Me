How to Fry Plaintains

1. Needed. You will need plaintains,of course. Not bananas, like what the Technical Writing Monkey eats, but plaintains
YOu will also need a frying pan, knife, and oil (I use vegetable oil).











2. Cut. Use your knife to cut your plantains, after peeling them. How you cut them is usually up to your preference
You can cut them to form circles or, as we usually do back in Jamaica, cut them diagonally to form what you see in the picture











3. Fry. Place your frying pan onto the stove and pour enough oil into the pan that rises about 1/2 an inch up the pan's sides
Once the oil has heated add your sliced plaintains. You will need to flip your plaintains to ensure neither side burns, until
both sides have reached golden perfect!










4. Enjoy! Plaintains can go with just about any other dish or by themselves as a dilicious snack.
